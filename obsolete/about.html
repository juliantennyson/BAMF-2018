BAMF! 2020 is the fourth installment of Bellingham Arts & Music Festival, originally created by the mutual madness of two Fairhaven College students at Western Washington University. Community is the driving force behind BAMF! We are committed to prioritizing local, Whatcom County artists of as many disciplines as we can find, and have forged some amazing partnerships and friendships because of this mission. We invite YOU to become a part of the BAMF! family—attendees included!

Here we are bringing BAMF! back again as a 2-day experience! Get ready for another, even-better double dose of creative wildness and courageous expression. This 48-hour format provides more space and time for both play and leisure, in addition to the high-quality art we’ve planted across the Quarry for your exploration. Take your time to take it all in!

Please peruse the website for information, tickets, and more!

Our Vision

Our vision is to present Bellingham with a confluence of local music, art, community, activism, business, camping, food, and more in an interactive festival setting to encourage celebration, growth, awareness, and inspire change in our little city. We aim to provide the best music and entertainment event possible in a way that is representative, inclusive, and affordable. BAMF! provides a stage and broad audience for local up-and-coming musicians, artists, and entrepreneurs. We work with students as well as both new and seasoned professionals to build bridges and instill confidence for success in their creative and career aspirations. We reject the notion of Subdued Excitement and encourage people to bring their whole selves with open minds and hearts to learn, explore, engage, and have fun with their community outside its traditional, physical boundaries.

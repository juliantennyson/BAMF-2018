BAMF! 2020 is the fourth annual Bellingham Arts & Music Festival, originally started by two students at Western Washington University’s Fairhaven College. The festival was created with this community in mind in order to prioritize as much local visual artists, performance artists, musicians, and other talent as we could possibly fit in the span of 24-hours.



We’re bringing the same love for local community, local arts, and the Lookout Arts Quarry’s unique atmosphere back again for a double dose and we couldn’t be more excited to see you there!

Please peruse the website for information, tickets, and more!

Our Vision

Our vision is to present Bellingham with a confluence of local music, art, activism, business, beer and food in an interactive festival setting to encourage celebration, growth, awareness, and inspire change in our little city. We aim to provide the best music and entertainment event possible in a way that is representative, inclusive, and affordable. BAMF! provides a stage and broad audience for local up-and-coming musicians, artists, and entrepreneurs. We work with students as well as both new and seasoned professionals to build bridges and instill confidence for success in their creative and career aspirations. We reject the notion of Subdued Excitement and encourage people to bring their whole selves with open minds and hearts to learn, explore, engage, and have fun with their community outside its traditional, physical boundaries.

BAMF! 2019 is the third annual Bellingham Arts & Music Festival, originally started by two students at Western Washington University’s Fairhaven College. The festival was created with this community in mind in order to prioritize as much local visual artists, performance artists, musicians, and other talent as we could possibly fit in the span of 24-hours.

New this year, BAMF! is expanding to a 2-day experience! We’ve heard many a great sigh about how quickly BAMF! comes and goes over the years, so we decided—why not?

We’re bringing the same love for local community, local arts, and the Lookout Arts Quarry’s unique atmosphere back again for a double dose and we couldn’t be more excited to see you there!

Please peruse the website for information, tickets, and more!

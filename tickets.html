Ticket Info

Tickets include access to the festival and camping, parking not included (please see Parking & Camping in the about section for more info).

Online ticket sales will close at 5pm Friday, May 11th, the night before BAMF! This is your last chance to purchase a ticket at the General Admission Tier 1 price level.

You can purchase tickets at the Box Office on-site starting at 12pm on Saturday, May 12th. We can accept cash or card. Tickets at the door will be General Admission Tier 2, $75.55 including fees and taxes.

Doors to the event open at 12pm on Saturday, May 12. You will NOT be allowed past the main entrance until 12pm. Do not come expecting to set up camp before 12pm.

If you have a promotional code for any ticket, it must be used by 5pm on Friday, May 11th.

All attendees need to pass through the Box Office to pay for parking and pick up or purchase wristbands. You must show print-at-home or physical tickets in exchange for a wristband.

Tickets will be available for day-of purchase at our Box Office from 12pm-9pm. We encourage online ticket purchase for quicker entry.

Only folks who have purchased tickets online will be allowed to enter the site past 9pm. Your wristbands will be available for pickup at the Info Booth inside the site.

Volunteers should pick up their wristbands from the Box Office and then follow the road straight into the site to Volunteer Check-In in order to say hello to the Volunteer Coordinator and head to your shift. Volunteers can arrive any time before their shift and still receive their wristbands.
